## 🏛️ THE PROPOSAL

### Digital Health Advice Accountability Act (DHAAA), 2026

India's first comprehensive legislative framework treating digital platforms as medical entities.

**6 Key Provisions:**

```
1. 📋 DIGITAL HEALTH ENTITY LICENSING (DHEL)
   ├── All platforms providing health advice must be licensed
   ├── Annual renewal conditional on accuracy benchmarks
   └── Penalty for operating without licence: ₹50 Crore+

2. 🔬 NMC-AI MEDICAL ACCURACY CERTIFICATION
   ├── Independent board of doctors + technologists
   ├── 90%+ accuracy required before deployment
   └── Annual re-certification mandatory

3. 🚨 MANDATORY EMERGENCY REFERRAL PROTOCOL
   ├── 47 red-flag symptoms trigger automatic warning
   ├── Warning displayed before any other content
   └── Violation = criminal negligence under IPC 304A

4. 🔒 HEALTH QUERY DATA PROTECTION
   ├── Your search queries = protected health data
   ├── Monetising health queries = cognizable offence
   └── Penalty: Up to ₹250 Crore per violation

5. ⚖️ DIGITAL MEDICAL LIABILITY FRAMEWORK
   ├── Platforms bear joint liability for harm caused
   ├── Fast-track Digital Medical Consumer Court
   └── 90-day case resolution mandate

6. 🚫 BAN ON PAID MEDICAL RANKINGS
   ├── Hospitals cannot buy higher search rankings
   ├── Rankings must reflect verified safety scores only
   └── Penalty: ₹10 Crore per paid placement found
```
