What CompetenceFirst Is NOT

❌ NOT a review platform       — patients cannot fake clinical competence
❌ NOT a paid listing service  — money cannot buy a higher score
❌ NOT self-reported data      — every parameter is externally verified  
❌ NOT another Practo          — Practo ranks by subscription, not skill
❌ NOT Google                  — Google ranks by advertising, not competence
❌ NOT geography limited       — India-wide, online-only launch

How It Works
For Patients

1. Search by condition / procedure / specialty
2. See doctors ranked by Competence Score — highest first
3. View full breakdown of every parameter — transparent
4. Filter by grade, qualification level, procedure volume
5. Book with confidence — not hope

For Doctors

1. Register free at competencefirst.in
2. Submit verified credentials — degree, NMC/DCI registration, procedure logs, CME
3. DHAF verification panel cross-checks within 7 working days
4. Competence Score generated — updated every 6 months
5. Ranked by merit — no advertising budget needed

For the Healthcare System

1. First public database of verified clinical competence in India
2. Incentivises doctors to pursue continuing education
3. Creates accountability for outcome data — currently hidden
4. Feeds directly into DHAAA 2026 compliance framework
5. Provides government with real-time competence data by district

The Regulatory Tailwind
The Digital Health Advice Accountability Act (DHAAA) 2026 — proposed by Dr Prakash Kumar — mandates:
✅ Public disclosure of doctor qualification data
✅ Mandatory outcome reporting by healthcare facilities
✅ Prohibition of paid medical search rankings
✅ Standardised safety data publication
When DHAAA passes — CompetenceFirst becomes the compliance infrastructure.
Every hospital, clinic, and health platform in India will need to publish the exact data CompetenceFirst already collects.
"We are building the platform before the law mandates it. First mover advantage is permanent and legally protected."
— Dr Prakash Kumar, Founder CompetenceFirst
